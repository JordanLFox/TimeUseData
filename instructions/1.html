Welcome to the Time Use Diary

The purpose of this exercise is to collect information on the activities you do.

This diary will involve you completing a short task in which you record the activities undertaken the previous day.

How the Diary Works

A full day will be shown hourly with 10 minutes intervals from 4am - 4am.

On your computer/laptop screen, the timeline appears horizontally, allowing you to scroll left and right through the day.

On your smartphone screen, the timeline appears vertically, allowing you to scroll up and down through the day.

Tips for Accurate Recording

  • Account for all your time - even short activities
  • If you did multiple things at once, record the main activity
  • Be as specific as possible when choosing activities
