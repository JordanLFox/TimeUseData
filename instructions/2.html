How to Use the Diary

1

Select an Activity

Choose an activity from the menu at the bottom of the screen.

Tap the (+) button in the bottom right corner to open the activity menu.

2

Place and Adjust Activity

Click where you want to place the activity, then drag the edges horizontally to set the duration.

Tap where you want to place the activity, then drag the edges vertically to set the duration.

3

Submit Your Diary

Once you've recorded all your activities for the day, click the Submit button to complete your diary.
