How the Diary Works

A full day will be shown hourly with 10 minutes intervals from 4am - 4am.

On your computer/laptop screen, the timeline appears horizontally, allowing you to scroll left and right through the day.

On your smartphone screen, the timeline appears vertically, allowing you to scroll up and down through the day.

In order to complete the diary please record what you did yesterday and answer some follow-up questions.

You have 48 hours from now in which to fill in the diary recording what activities you did yesterday.

You will be asked to provide the following information:

  • Primary activity: your main activity
  • Secondary activity: any activity you were doing at the same time as the primary activity
  • Where were you at the time: at home, at work or in some other place
  • Who you were with: combinations of e.g. spouse, children, non-household members, their gender and age group, and your degree of contact with them

Navigating through the diary:

Starting from 4am, please click on an option from the bottom of the page and drag the pointer horizontally to select the time during which you carried out that activity.

Starting from 4am, please click on an option from the bottom of the page and drag the pointer vertically to select the time during which you carried out that activity.

Once a row has been completed, press 'next' to move on to the next topic. You can:

  • Edit rows at any stage throughout the diary
  • Increase or reduce time spent on each activity
  • Delete an answer or entire row

Once a column has been completed, press 'next' to move on to the next topic. You can:

  • Edit columns at any stage throughout the diary
  • Increase or reduce time spent on each activity
  • Delete an answer or entire column
